OVERVIEW

Experience:

I am a Lead Interaction Designer, Service Architect and Digital Product Strategist.

My work includes a broad mix of web-based SAAS applications for Fortune 1000 companies as well as concept-stage start-ups.

I really enjoy working on software that requires the creation and adoption of accompanying business processes.

My experience spans a particulary broad set of vertical concerns including Finance, Consumer Banking, Travel, Hospitality, Pharma, Beverage, Retail, Education and Government.

Additionally, I have a unique perspective on the relationship between advertising agencies and media having worked as both a service provider and client of major digital agencies.
